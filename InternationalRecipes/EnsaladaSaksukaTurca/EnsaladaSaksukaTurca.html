Arroz Turco

If you want to prepare a different dish, with traditional ingredients and surprise everyone with the exquisite flavor of a Turkish recipe, here we tell you how you can easily prepare a Turkish Saksuka Salad.

Recipe:

  • 500 grams of rice
  • 400 grams of lamb meat
  • 1 cup lamb or vegetable broth
  • 3 medium carrots
  • 2 red or green bell peppers
  • 3 big onions
  • 1 medium zucchini
  • 100 grams of raisins
  • Cumin
  • Salt

Preparation:

1. Prepare the rice in the traditional way and reserve.

2. Cut the lamb into small pieces, in the large frying pan where you will add all the ingredients, drip oil and over high heat seal the lamb.

3. Cut the vegetables into thin julienne strips and add them to the pan and at the end add the raisins, stir and season the mixture to your liking, also add the cumin and rosemary, mix very well so that all the flavors are incorporated.

4. Add the rice and the lamb stock, stir and rectify the flavor. Cover the pan and cook until the broth dries.

5. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes and serve.
