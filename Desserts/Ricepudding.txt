Ingredientes:


1 cup of rice
1 liter of milk
Clove
Sugar
Ground cinnamon


Preparation:
firstable, put the milk in a pot and add the well-washed rice, letting it to soak for an hour or more.
After that put it over medium heat with the cinnamon and the clove until it boils.
Then lower the heat and let it cook until the rice becomes creamy and then add sugar as much as you want.
Now you can remove it from the heat and serve it with a sprinkle of cinnamon powder.
