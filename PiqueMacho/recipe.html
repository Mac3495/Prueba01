Pique Macho Cochabambino

Recipe:

  • 1/2 kilo soft meat loin or pulp
  • 6 Vienna sausage
  • 6 white potatoes
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 Green Locoto
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 tablespoons Soy sauce
  • 1 can Beer
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Preparation:

1. Peel and chop the potatoes into 4 large pieces. Cut the tomato into large slices. Chop the onion into thin julienne

2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a very hot frying pan, place the meat cut into cubes and seasoned with enough black pepper and little salt, along with the onion, fry the meat until it releases its juices and then add the soy sauce and half a can of beer. When the meat is cooked and has taken color cut the sausages into slices and fry them together with the meat

3. Fry the potatoes in hot oil, take them off the heat and serve on a plate, place the meat and sausage preparation with its juices on top and finally serve with chopped tomato and finely chopped locoto. It can also be decorated with hard-boiled egg.

4. Enjoy a delicious Bolivian and Cochabamba classic
